## Notes

- Two-CTA pattern (subscribe now vs. book a call) captures both ready-to-buy and needs-nurturing visitors
- "Pause or cancel anytime" at $10K/month is a strong risk reversal — makes the price feel less permanent
- Client list includes recognizable consumer names (Wish, Linktree) alongside crypto/AI-native brands — signals range
- No pricing tiers — one price, take it or leave it; positions them as high-conviction about their value
- "20 years in the game" + "millions of users" anchors credibility before the price reveal
- Portfolio is visual-first, no case study copy — lets work speak, keeps the page fast to consume
