# hurdle.studio

**URL:** https://hurdle.studio  
**Type:** Design studio — subscription model for early-stage founders

---

## Hook

> "We help early stage founders accelerate their design efforts."

Direct problem-solution framing for a specific audience (early-stage founders). "Accelerate" signals speed, which the comparison table reinforces (24–48h delivery vs. agency delays).

---

## Content Outline

### 1. Hero
- **Headline:** "We help early stage founders accelerate their design efforts."
- **Subheadline:** "Take your next leap with us, we're a design studio with over 10 years of experience crafting delightful user experiences for start-ups, founders and established brands and businesses."
- **Body:** Describes service from wireframes to full-fidelity designs, emphasizing product-market fit and customer connection
- **CTAs:** "Book an intro call ↗" / "Email"

### 2. Pricing Package
- **Price:** $6,000/month
- **Features:**
  - 5 days a week
  - 1 design lead (10-year veteran)
  - One request at a time
  - Unlimited design
  - Dedicated Slack channel
  - Unlimited revisions
- **Note:** "Pause or cancel anytime"

### 3. Social Proof
> "I have had the pleasure of working with Kyler for the last year and a half and he is one of the most talented designers I have ever worked with."  
> — John Grange, Software Engineer at Apple

### 4. Services
Three offerings, each with "Schedule a call" CTA:
1. **Product Design** — UI, navigation, core flow, basic branding
2. **Branding & Identity** — Logo, digital direction (colors, fonts, imagery)
3. **Website Development** — Framer or Webflow builds

### 5. Comparison Table
- "Hurdle vs traditional agency"
- 24–48h delivery vs. industry-standard delays

### 6. FAQ
- How quickly can you start?
- What if I don't like the designs?
- Why not hire a designer?
- How much design work will I get?
- What software is used?

### 7. Footer CTA
- **Headline:** "Ready to take the leap?"
- **Body:** Explains the decision difficulty, invites email signup for work samples
- **CTA:** "Send me work sample"

- "Pause or cancel anytime" directly neutralizes the main objection to a $6K/mo subscription
