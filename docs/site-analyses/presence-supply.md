- Zero social proof by design — FAQ explicitly addresses their "no reviews" philosophy

- Scarcity is baked in structurally (limited edition, sold out at launch)
- Pricing shown upfront ($100) — unusual for luxury/artisan products, signals confidence
- "Next: January 2025" teases continuity without over-promising
- The whole page reads as an object of design itself, not just a product listing
